Our Story हाम्रो कथा

Welcome to Lete Thakali Kitchen, a modest yet inviting restaurant that takes pride in serving authentic Thakali dishes with an emphasis on flavor and homely comfort. Our recipes originate from the Thak Khola region of Mustang, bringing the unique tastes of the Himalayas to your plate. With multiple locations in Tangal, Dhumbarahi, and Manbhawan, we have been serving families, travelers, and food enthusiasts who seek genuine Nepali flavors. लेटे थकाली किचनमा स्वागत छ, एक साधारण तर आकर्षक रेस्टुरेन्ट जसले स्वाद र घरेलु आराममा जोड दिँदै प्रामाणिक थकाली व्यञ्जनहरू प्रदान गर्नमा गर्व गर्छ। हाम्रा व्यञ्जनहरू मुस्ताङको थक खोला क्षेत्रबाट उत्पत्ति भएका छन्।
